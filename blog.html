Projects with NGOs

We will be primarily collaborating with NGOs like Pratisandhi which works to create awareness about sex-ed and work on some social welfare projects related to sex education, health and hygiene, or any other general social issues faced by the community. We will also collaborate with them and call volunteers from campus for the local NGOs nearby specially during the breaks so as to increase the social work and volunteerism in campus.

TIMELINE: Once in a quarter - overall at least three such collaborations
